A bit about me

I'm Arnav, currently in my third year of BCA with a specialisation in AI and ML at UPES Dehradun. I got into coding properly in my first year and kind of never stopped since then.

Most of what I know comes from building stuff — not just watching tutorials. My two main projects are a face recognition attendance system (it replaced manual roll calls at my institution) and an AI plant care app that diagnoses plant diseases from photos. Both are actually deployed and working, which I think matters more than just having code on GitHub.
